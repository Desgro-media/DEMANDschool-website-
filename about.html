HOW WE STARTED

Built By Practitioners, Not Professors

Demand School started with a simple frustration: traditional education wasn't built for how Marketing, AI and Content actually work today. Course outlines were years behind the platforms and tools students were expected to use on day one of a real job. So instead of another lecture-and-textbook program, we built cohorts run by people who are still doing the work — planning campaigns, shooting reels, running ad accounts, and shipping AI-powered content for real clients — and put students through the same process, with mentors reviewing every step.

OUR MISSION

Bridging the Gap

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry needs by delivering practical, market-driven programs in Marketing, AI, Content Creation, and emerging technologies. Through expert mentorship, hands-on learning, internships, and career support, we empower learners to build successful and sustainable careers — not just collect a certificate and hope it's enough.
